Simple Marketing Reporting for Local Businesses (Weekly Routine)

By BUXI DIGITALDecember 15, 20257 min read

If reporting feels complicated, you’re tracking too much. Small teams need a simple weekly routine that points to the next fix.

Quick Start: the 4 numbers to track

- Leads (calls + forms) - Response time - Booked rate - Close rate

How to run a 20-minute weekly review

Step 1: count leads by source

Website, referrals, etc. Keep it simple.

Step 2: check response time

Fast response wins. If it’s slow, fix the intake workflow first.

Step 3: identify one bottleneck

Too few leads? Improve pages/SEO. Too many unqualified? Fix messaging and forms. Lots of quotes but low close? Fix follow-up.

Action Box: reporting checklist

- Track the 4 numbers weekly - Write one “fix this next” note - Improve one page or workflow per week - Keep a clean conversion path to Contact - Use content to support services (Blog)

Example scenario

A local business realizes they have enough leads, but response time is slow. They tighten follow-up and bookings increase without spending more on marketing.
