Contact Page That Converts (Local Lead Checklist)

By BUXI DIGITALDecember 15, 20257 min read

Your contact page isn’t a form. It’s the moment where a visitor decides if you’re easy to work with.

Quick Start (10 minutes)

1) Put one clear headline: “Request a quote” / “Book an appointment.”
2) Add response expectations: “We reply within 1 business day.”
3) Make phone number and email clickable.

The contact page template

1) A clear promise

Tell people what happens after they submit: who replies, how fast, and what you’ll ask.

2) One short form (6–10 fields max)

Ask only what you need to respond. If you need more details, collect them after the first reply.

3) Proof and reassurance

Add 2–3 reviews and a short “what to expect” section.

4) Alternate contact options

Include phone and email. Don’t force one channel.

Action Box: contact page checklist

- Clear headline and CTA - Form is short and easy on mobile - Response-time promise is visible - Phone number is tappable - Confirmation message is clear - Links back to Services and About

Example scenario

A local business reduces form fields and adds “We reply within 1 business day.” Form completions increase because visitors feel safer.
