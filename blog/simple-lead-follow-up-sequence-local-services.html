Simple Lead Follow-Up Sequence (Local Services)

By BUXI DIGITALDecember 15, 20258 min read

Most leads don’t say “no.” They go quiet. A consistent follow-up sequence recovers those leads without being pushy.

Quick Start (copy this)

Use this simple timeline: Day 0: confirm request + next step
Day 1: call or short check-in Day 3: answer common questions Day 7: final “close the loop” message

Message templates

Day 0: confirmation

“Thanks for reaching out—got it. We’ll respond within 1 business day. If it’s urgent, call us at 306-874-7017.”

Day 1: simple check-in

“Quick check-in—when’s the best time to connect about your request?”

Day 3: helpful info

“To help you plan: most projects like this take [time range] depending on scope. Want us to confirm a quote?”

Day 7: close the loop

“Last note from us—do you still want help with this, or should we close your request for now?”

Action Box: follow-up checklist

- Respond fast (speed-to-lead) - Assign a lead owner - Use a 4-touch sequence - Keep messages short and respectful - Always include a clear next step - Link back to Contact and Services

Example scenario

A local service business increases booked calls by following up on quotes consistently—without sounding aggressive—because leads feel supported.
