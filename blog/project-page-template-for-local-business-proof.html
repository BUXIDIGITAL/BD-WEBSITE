Project Page Template (Proof That Converts)

By BUXI DIGITALDecember 15, 20257 min read

Your portfolio shouldn’t be a gallery. It should be proof that builds trust and helps customers choose you.

Quick Start

Take one completed job and write a short project page using the template below.

The project page template

1) The result (headline)

Example: “Fixed the problem in 2 days with minimal downtime.”

2) The situation

What was wrong and what the customer needed.

3) The plan

How you approached it and what options you considered.

4) Photos or proof

Use a few optimized photos and add captions (what we’re seeing and why it matters).

5) Timeline and scope

Keep it clear. People want to know what “normal” looks like.

6) CTA

Invite the visitor to take the next step: request a quote.

Action Box: project page checklist

- Clear outcome headline - Before/after or proof - Short scope and timeline - What made it challenging (1 sentence) - Customer quote or review - CTA to Contact

Example scenario

A local contractor posts one project page per week. Prospects mention specific projects on calls because proof is easy to find.
