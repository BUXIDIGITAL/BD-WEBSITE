Local SEO Content Plan for Service Businesses (12 Topics)

By BUXI DIGITALDecember 15, 20259 min read

The best local SEO content answers real customer questions and supports your service pages—not random “trend” posts.

Quick Start

Start with 3 content types: 1) Service pages 2) Service area pages 3) FAQs / project posts

12 topic ideas that drive local leads

- Pricing ranges (with disclaimers) - Timelines (“How long does it take?”) - “What to expect” process posts - Maintenance and prevention tips - Common mistakes customers make - Before/after case examples - Service area guides - Comparison posts (“Option A vs B”) - Seasonal checklists - Warranty/guarantee explanations - Materials/quality explainers - “Do I need a permit?” style FAQs

Internal linking rule (simple)

Every post should link to: Services and Contact. Add one link to About when relevant.

Action Box: content plan checklist

- Pick your top 3 services - Create service pages that convert - Add 5–10 quality service area pages - Publish 1 helpful post per week (FAQ or case) - Link posts back to Services and Contact - Add proof (reviews/photos) regularly

Example scenario

A local service business publishes one FAQ per week tied to their main services. Calls improve because pages match real searches and the site becomes more trustworthy.
